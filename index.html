Privacy Policy

Word Ladder — Android App

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Word Ladder ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you play the game on your Android device. We are committed to transparency and to complying with applicable privacy laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR.

By using Word Ladder you agree to the practices described here. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we will ask for your consent before showing personalised advertisements.

2. Information we collect

Word Ladder itself does not require an account and does not collect your name, email, or other directly identifying information. The following data may be collected:

Game progress (stored locally on your device)

Your puzzle chains, best scores, daily-challenge streaks, and hint tokens are stored in your device's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

Advertising data (Google AdMob)

To display ads, Google AdMob may collect your Advertising ID (GAID), IP address, device model, OS version, coarse location, and interaction data (clicks, impressions). This is used to serve ads and measure their performance. If you are in the EEA/UK, personalised ads are only shown after you give your consent.

Analytics (Firebase / AdMob SDK)

Aggregated, anonymised crash reports and usage metrics may be collected to help us improve stability. No individual user is identified.

3. Advertising — Google AdMob

Word Ladder is free to play and is supported by advertising provided by Google AdMob (a service of Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA).

  • AdMob may use the Android Advertising ID (GAID) to personalise ads.
  • AdMob uses cookies and similar technologies on web-based ad creatives.

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR — EEA / UK users)

If you are in the EEA or UK, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — personalised advertising and ad-measurement cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — aggregated analytics to improve app stability and fix crashes, where these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — storing local game progress so the app functions correctly.

We use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK to collect and manage GDPR consent within the app for EEA/UK users.
